Prime Tower

The skyscraper of Zurich

The Primetower is a skyscraper in Zurich. It has a heigh of 126m and was the highest building in switzerland until 2015. This building was built in 2011 but the whole planing and executioon took 15 years and over 110 million CHF. The Primetower is mainly used for work offices, though in the building you can find a conference center, a restaurant on the top floor and on the ground floor the Hotel Rivington & Sons.
